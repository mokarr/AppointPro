## Managing Your Profile

This article explains how to update your profile information, change your password, and manage your preferences.

### Update Profile Information

Go to "Settings" -> "Profile" to update your name, email, and other personal details.

### Change Password

For security, regularly change your password. You can do this in "Settings" -> "Security" -> "Change Password".
