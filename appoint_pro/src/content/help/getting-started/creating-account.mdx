# Creating Your AppointPro Account

Follow these simple steps to create your AppointPro account and start managing your appointments with ease.

## Step 1: Sign Up

Navigate to our [Sign Up page](/signup) and fill in the required details, including your email address and a strong password.

## Step 2: Email Verification

After signing up, a verification email will be sent to your provided address. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

Once verified, you'll be guided to complete your business profile. This includes:

*   Your business name
*   Contact information
*   Business category
*   Your business hours

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during the sign-up process, please refer to our [FAQ section](/help/faq) or contact our support team.
