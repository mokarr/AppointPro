## Managing Your Team

Learn how to invite new team members to your AppointPro account and set their roles and permissions.

### Invite Team Members

Go to "Team" -> "Members" -> "Invite New Member" and enter their email address.

### Assign Roles and Permissions

Define what each team member can access and modify by assigning appropriate roles.
